If you have an interest in helping us out, or contributing code, we would be grateful for your assistance. To contribute code or anything else to out mod project, you must first agree and sign our Contributor License Agreement. This allows us to use your code without restriction, and ensures that you get credited for what you make! [Take a look at our CLA here.](https://gist.github.com/spikespaz/9f5b1f3d5c7a54965678292026515ec3#essential-additions-cla) You may also sign it ahead of time with CLA Assistant, though this is not necessary as you will have an opportunity to do so when you submit your pull request. To read and sign our CLA, go [here](https://cla-assistant.io/spikespaz/EssentialAdditions). Contributing

This project is open source, so we embrace contributions and suggestions. You can help out in several ways, either by reporting bugs to us on GitHub, or forking the mod and helping us out with the code. Report bugs at the Issues tab at the top.
